# Assumptions & Constraints

## Platform Assumptions

- Posting APIs may not exist
- Platform terms must be respected
- No scraping or bypassing restrictions

---

## Scope Assumptions

- Internal tool only
- Single brand in V1
- LinkedIn-style posts initially
- Low to moderate posting volume

---

## Human Assumptions

- Humans make mistakes
- Reviewers may act concurrently
- Approvals must be auditable

---

## AI Assumptions

- LLMs hallucinate
- Output is probabilistic
- Prompts alone are insufficient for safety

---

## Tradeoffs

Chosen:
- Safety over speed
- Determinism over intelligence
- Explainability over ML complexity

Deferred:
- Embedding-based retrieval
- Auto-approval
- Full engagement analytics
